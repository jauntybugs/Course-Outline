Common Interview Questions

  • "How did you validate your design decisions?"

Behavioral Questions

  • "How do you handle feedback and criticism?"

  • Practice design exercises daily (even 15 minutes)
  • Build a design inspiration collection
  • Join UX communities and engage actively
  • Seek feedback early and often
  • Document your learning journey
  • Network with classmates and industry professionals

Time Management

Weekly Schedule Recommendation

Classes
Wed (Virtual) + Fri & Sat (Physical) - 9 hours total per week
Practice
Daily practice sessions: 30-60 minutes of design work
Reading
2-3 hours weekly: Industry articles, case studies, UX books

5-8 hours weekly: Assignment work and portfolio development

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

Perfectionism

Focus on iteration over perfection

Isolation

Collaborate and seek feedback regularly

Theory Only

Balance learning with hands-on practice

Procrastination

Break projects into smaller, manageable tasks
